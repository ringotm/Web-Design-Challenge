Summary: Latitude vs. X

This project analyzes the relationships of weather characteristics vs. latitude for cities across the globe. A random distribution of cities spanning the entire range of latitude and longitudes was chosen so as to get an unbiased assessment. Weather data was then pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API. The weather parameters chosen to investigate were maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.

After data was pulled in from the API, the matplotlib library within python was used to create scatter plots to visually investigate the relationships. Separate pages are provided for each visualization along w/ a short description. The source data is also provided as a table. Keep in mind the date the data was pulled (06/15/20) when making conclusions as weather patterns can be affected by seasonality.
